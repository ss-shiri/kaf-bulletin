About this bulletin

Headlines are collected on a schedule from open sources in twelve languages, using a hand-built lexicon of chemical and biological terms in each language. Items are stored exactly as published, in their own script and direction. Appearing here is not an endorsement, a verification, or a judgement of accuracy. Subject labels come from matching terms in the headline itself and are marked unconfirmed when the match came from the article body instead. Follow the link and assess the source yourself.
